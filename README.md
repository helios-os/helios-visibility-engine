# Helios Visibility Engine

Helios Visibility Engine is a core component of Helios OS.

It focuses on one problem:

How businesses can become **understandable and recommendable for AI systems.**

As discovery shifts from traditional search engines to AI assistants, the way companies become visible is fundamentally changing.

Helios Visibility Engine explores the frameworks and structures that allow businesses to be interpreted by AI systems.

---

## The Problem

Traditional SEO optimized websites for search engines.

AI systems operate differently.

AI models recommend companies based on structured signals they can interpret.

If a business cannot be interpreted by AI systems, it risks becoming invisible.

---

## Approach

Helios Visibility Engine explores three layers of AI visibility:

### Business Understanding

Mapping real expertise, services and capabilities.

### Structured Knowledge

Transforming business knowledge into machine-readable structures.

### AI Recommendation Readiness

Ensuring AI systems can interpret and recommend the business.

---

## Part of Helios OS

Helios Visibility Engine is one of the core modules within the Helios OS ecosystem.

Helios OS explores infrastructure that allows businesses to remain visible in the AI era.
